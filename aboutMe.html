ABOUT US

The Secret Life of Shoes, a shoe selling company, located in Irvine, California. It is a company that combine "research, produce, sale and service" as one. Although it was formally established in 2015, it grew up in a short time and became a large scale shoe manufacturer. The plant covers an area of 800 square meters, more than 100 employees and a management team with 5 people. The main production of our company is woman’s shoes, including scandals, boots, etc. Except that, we also provide men’s shoes, like sneakers, loafers, etc. With thousands of shoes, great prices, and an ever-changing inventory, we inspire passionate shoe lovers nationwide. At the same time, The Secret Life of shoes also keeps developing the new shoes with our own designers, who graduate from famous school of design, in order to let our customers always catch up the latest fashion.
